Should I get solar panels for my business?

Benefits of Transitioning to Solar Panels

REDUCED OPERATING COSTS

Solar Panel Energy will reduce your building's electricity bill. The money that is saved on your energy bills will have a large impact on your overall business.

FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE THROUGH GOVERNMENT GRANTS

Government Incentives such as the Better Energy Communities mean the use of solar energy technology is a good financial decision by companies. Investing in commercial solar panels will generate long-term savings and quick payback.

MAINTENANCE FREE

With a solar system installation you have no mechanically moving parts, this means less breakages and less maintenance. The solar energy system will generate electricity cleanly and quietly.

PROTECT YOUR BUSINESS AGAINST FUTURE PRICE CHANGES

With a solar system installation you have control and are protecting your business against any future price increases. Your business will no longer be dependent on external electricity prices and the changes within the market.

GAIN GREEN BUSINESS CREDENTIALS

Solar Panel Technology means that your company uses less fossil fuel. You will reduce your business's greenhouse gas emissions and pollution. Being a sustainable business can be positive for public relations and a good marketing tool for your business.

Available Commercial Solar Grants

Non-Domestic Microgen Grant (NDMG)

On July 4th 2023, the Irish government announced that business owners will receive additional support through the NDMG funding.

New funding ranges from €2,700 to €162,600 to support an even wider range of businesses to switch to solar. This will typically support 20-30% of the investment cost, reducing payback to as little as 5 years.

Check out these remarkable savings you can make with Solar PV grants:

  • For a 50kWp system, you can save a whopping €12,600!
  • If you opt for a 100kWp system, you could enjoy savings of €22,600. That's a significant boost for your bottom line!
  • For even greater benefits, consider a 150kWp system and save a staggering €32,600!
  • There’s more! You can now install a 1MWp system and receive an amazing grant amount of €162,600! This is an extraordinary opportunity for businesses with higher energy needs.
