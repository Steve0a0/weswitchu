Farmers

Electricity prices have finally started to come down. Are you taking advantage of this? Sign up once with WeSwitchU and we will ensure that you are always on the best rates for your specific usage profile.

A dairy farmer with circa 100 cows can save up to €3,000, on a house can save up to €600, in the current market by switching electricity suppliers.

How it works:

  1. Fill in your details below. We will get in touch to request your latest electricity bills and your meter readings.
  2. We will contact you with your usage analysis and switch options.
  3. With your approval, we will process your first switch.
  4. We switch you every year thereafter, or as soon as a better deal becomes available – you don't have to do anything!
