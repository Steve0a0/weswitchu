How it works

WeSwitchU is a paid, premium switching service with a simple mission – to save customers time and money on their energy bills. Customers only need to sign up once and WeSwitchU will continuously switch them to the cheapest energy tariff based on their usage.

Sign Up Once

Create an account and sign up for your first switch. All you need is:

  • A recent electricity and/or gas bill
  • Your meter reading(s)
  • Your IBAN details for payments

Switch to a New Deal

For a small fee of 10% of your savings (min €40), we'll review your selection and process your switch to your new supplier. We will then monitor the energy market year-round to ensure you're on the best deal.

WeSwitchU, every year

WeSwitchU will find and switch you to a better tariff the following year (and every year thereafter!) before you come out of contract, ensuring you never get caught on expensive out-of-contract rates.

It doesn't have to stop at the switch...

We'll also help you to make improvements to your home's energy efficiency and connect you to our directory of both local and national contractors.
