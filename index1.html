HOW DOES IT WORK

Sign Up Once

Create an account and sign up for your first switch. All you need is your latest bill, your meter readings, and your IBAN details for payments.

Switch to a New Deal

For a small fee of 10% of your savings (min €40), we'll review your selection and process your switch to your new supplier.

WeSwitchU, every year

WeSwitchU will find and switch you to a better tariff each year, ensuring you never get caught on expensive out-of-contract rates.
